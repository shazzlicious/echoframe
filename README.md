# EchoFrame

### Transforming Cultural Stories into Creator-Ready Content with Generative AI

EchoFrame is an AI-powered storytelling platform that bridges cultural heritage and modern content creation. It transforms literature, poetry, folklore, historical narratives, and regional stories into creator-ready digital assets including scripts, storyboards, visual prompts, captions, hashtags, and reel concepts.

**Theme:** Generative AI & Creative Technology

---

## Problem Statement

Cultural stories, poetry, folklore, and historical narratives are rich sources of knowledge and creativity, yet they often remain inaccessible to modern digital audiences.

Creators spend significant time manually converting long narratives into scripts, storyboards, captions, and visual concepts suitable for social media. Existing AI tools can generate text, but they lack cultural understanding, emotional context, and structured storytelling workflows.

As a result, valuable stories struggle to reach new generations through modern content formats.

---

## Our Solution

EchoFrame uses Generative AI to understand a story's narrative structure, cultural significance, emotional tone, and historical context before transforming it into multiple creator-ready outputs.

With a single input, users can generate:

* Scripts
* Storyboards
* Visual Prompts
* Captions
* Hashtags
* Reel Concepts

This enables creators to transform stories into engaging digital experiences faster and more effectively.

## EchoLens: Cultural Intelligence Engine

EchoLens is the signature innovation behind EchoFrame.

When a user uploads a story, poem, folktale, or historical narrative, EchoLens automatically identifies:

* Era
* Region
* Cultural Significance
* Emotional Tone
* Key Characters
* Narrative Themes
* Visual Motifs
* Historical Context

This helps preserve meaning while adapting stories for modern content creation.
